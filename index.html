Privacy Policy Warm Vinyl Moments · Last updated: April 21, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how the WarmVinylMoments automation tool ("the App") handles information when it interacts with third-party platforms on behalf of the Warm Vinyl Moments YouTube channel. What the App Does The App is a personal, single-user automation tool that publishes content to social platforms (including Pinterest) when a new video is released on the Warm Vinyl Moments YouTube channel. It creates pins containing the video thumbnail, title, and a link to the YouTube video. Data We Collect The App does not collect any personal data from users or visitors. It does not have a public-facing interface and is not accessible to the general public. Data We Access on Third-Party Platforms The App uses write-only access to Pinterest. It creates pins on behalf of the channel owner. It does not read, store, analyze, or share any Pinterest user data, follower data, or engagement data. Data Storage The only data stored locally is the URL of published pins, saved in a private SQLite database on a single personal computer. This data is never shared with third parties. Third-Party Services The App interacts with the Pinterest API under the Pinterest Developer Guidelines. No data obtained through the Pinterest API is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes. Children's Privacy The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13. Changes to This Policy This policy may be updated occasionally.
